How It All Started

Russo's Ristorante, located in New York City, serves classic and fresh meals that combine the best of traditional Italian recipes with fresh ingredients.

Opened in 1985, Russo's is celebrated as one of the oldest family-owned and operated restaurants running in Midtown Manhattan serving the cherished Italian traditions as it's main ingredients. Russo's menu pairs generations of authenticity and love with freshness and flavor

Sit back, relax and enjoy as we invite you to join us for our brand of Italian hospitality.
